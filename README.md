## 📌 Campaign Subscription Prediction

This project analyzes bank marketing campaign data to predict whether a customer will subscribe to a term deposit.

The goal is to understand customer behavior and improve marketing efficiency using data-driven decision making.

---

## 🔍 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning

* Removed duplicates
* Checked data consistency
* Handled unknown values

---

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis

Analyzed:

* Demographics
* Financial status
* Loan information
* Contact methods
* Campaign timing

Visualizations were used to understand patterns related to campaign success.

---

### 3. Business Insights Exploration

Examined how factors such as:

* Job type
* Marital status
* Education
* Loans
* Contact channel

affect subscription outcomes.

---

### 4. Outlier Analysis

Outliers were investigated rather than automatically removed to avoid losing meaningful behavioral patterns.

---

### 5. Target Understanding

Studied success vs failure distribution to support classification modeling.

---

## 📊 Objective

Build a predictive understanding of:

* Who is likely to subscribe
* What factors influence decisions
* How marketing strategy can be optimized

---

## 🚀 Use Cases

* Marketing optimization
* Customer segmentation
* Campaign targeting
* Financial product adoption prediction
